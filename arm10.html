What are some unblocked games?

Agar.io

Agar.io is a popular multiplayer game that can be played online. In this game, you start as a small cell and you need to eat other cells to grow. As you grow, you can also split your cell into smaller pieces to avoid being eaten by other players. The goal of the game is to become the largest cell on the map.

Run 3

Run 3 is a popular endless runner game that can be played online. In this game, you control a character who runs through a tunnel in space. The tunnel is filled with obstacles and you need to jump, run, and slide to avoid them.

Cat Ninja

Cat Ninja is a platformer game that can be played online. In this game, you control a ninja cat who needs to navigate through levels filled with traps and obstacles. The game has a cartoonish art style and a catchy soundtrack.

Tetris

Tetris is a classic puzzle game that has been around for decades. The goal of the game is to stack falling blocks in a way that creates complete lines. Once a line is complete, it disappears and you earn points. The game becomes more difficult as the blocks fall faster and the shapes become more complex.

Tank Trouble

Tank Trouble is a multiplayer game that can be played online. In this game, you control a tank and you need to destroy your opponents by shooting them with your cannon.

2048

2048 is a simple puzzle game that can be played online or on a mobile device. The goal of the game is to combine tiles with the same number to create a tile with the number 2048. The game is easy to learn but difficult to master, as the board quickly becomes filled with tiles.

Slope

Slope is an endless runner game that can be played online. In this game, you control a ball that rolls down a slope filled with obstacles. The goal of the game is to reach the end of the slope without falling off or hitting an obstacle.

Super Smash Flash 2

Super Smash Flash 2 is a fan-made fighting game that can be played online. The game has a wide range of characters and stages, as well as a variety of game modes.

Pac-Man

In this game, you control Pac-Man, a yellow circle who needs to eat all the dots in a maze while avoiding ghosts. The game becomes more difficult as the ghosts become faster and more challenging to avoid. The game has simple graphics and intuitive controls, making it a timeless classic that is still enjoyed by many today.
